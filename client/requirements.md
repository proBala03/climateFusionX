## Packages
recharts | Complex data visualization for historical and forecast data with confidence intervals
framer-motion | Smooth page transitions and futuristic staggered reveal animations
clsx | Utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | Utility to merge tailwind classes without style conflicts

## Notes
The application features a dark futuristic "glassmorphism" aesthetic.
Charts combine historical data (solid line) with forecast data (dashed line) and confidence bounds (shaded area).
